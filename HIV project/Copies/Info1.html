HIV/AIDS


The Human Immunodeficiancy Virus and Aquired Immune Deficiancy Syndrome are two types of STDs that can destroy your immune system. HIV is a virus that spreads throughout your body and attacks the CD4 or T cells. These are special cells that help fight off different pathogens that enter the body. But when the virus is destrying these cells, it makes it much harder for the body to fight off other infections, pathogens, and diseases. When this goes untreated, this can develope into AIDS which mean that your entire immune system is weakened and ridded of most of the T cells. You are more likly to catch other illnesses because of that.
